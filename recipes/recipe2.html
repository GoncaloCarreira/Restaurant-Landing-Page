Beef Cheeseburger

The classic taste of a juicy beef patty topped with melted cheese in a soft bun.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 slices of your favorite cheese (cheddar, American, Swiss, etc.)
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Sliced red onions
  • Ketchup and mustard for condiments

Instructions:

  1. Divide the ground beef into four equal portions and shape them into burger patties. Season with salt and pepper.
  2. Preheat the grill or a skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Cook the burger patties for about 4-5 minutes per side or until they reach your desired level of doneness.
  4. In the last minute of cooking, place a slice of cheese on each patty and cover to let it melt.
  5. Toast the hamburger buns on the grill or in a toaster.
  6. Assemble the burgers by placing a lettuce leaf on the bottom half of each bun, followed by a burger patty with melted cheese.
  7. Top with sliced tomatoes and red onions. Add ketchup and mustard, then cover with the other half of the bun.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious homemade cheeseburgers!
