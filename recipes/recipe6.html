Strawberry and Apple Pancakes

Fluffy pancakes topped with fresh strawberries, apple slices, honey, cinnamon, and crunchy walnuts.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup fresh strawberries, sliced
  • 1 apple, thinly sliced
  • Honey, for drizzling
  • Ground cinnamon, for sprinkling
  • 1/2 cup walnuts, chopped

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together buttermilk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Let the batter rest for a few minutes.
  4. Heat a griddle or non-stick pan over medium heat. Spoon the pancake batter onto the griddle and cook until bubbles form on the surface. Flip and cook the other side until golden brown.
  5. Repeat until all the batter is used, making a stack of pancakes.
  6. Top the pancakes with sliced strawberries, apple slices, and chopped walnuts.
  7. Drizzle honey over the pancakes and sprinkle with ground cinnamon.
  8. Serve warm and enjoy your Strawberry and Apple Pancakes!
