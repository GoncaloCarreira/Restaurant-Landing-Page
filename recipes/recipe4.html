Avocado and Tomato Salad

A refreshing and nutritious vegetarian salad featuring avocado, tomato, corn, and fresh parsley.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe avocados, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup corn kernels, cooked and cooled
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine diced avocados, cherry tomatoes, corn, and chopped parsley.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together lime juice, olive oil, ground cumin, salt, and black pepper to create the dressing.
  3. Pour the dressing over the salad ingredients and gently toss to coat evenly.
  4. Chill the salad in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes to let the flavors meld.
  5. Serve the avocado and tomato salad chilled, and enjoy the burst of fresh flavors!
