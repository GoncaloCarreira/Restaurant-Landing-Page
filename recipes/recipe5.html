Grilled Salmon and Avocado Salad

Grilled salmon combined with crisp lettuce, ripe tomatoes, creamy avocado, and a zesty lemon dressing.

Ingredients:

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 8 cups mixed lettuce greens
  • 2 ripe avocados, sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Juice of 2 lemons
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Season the salmon fillets with salt, black pepper, and olive oil.
  3. Grill the salmon for 4-5 minutes per side or until cooked through and flaky.
  4. In a large bowl, combine the mixed lettuce greens, sliced avocados, and cherry tomatoes.
  5. Place grilled salmon on top of the salad.
  6. In a small bowl, whisk together lemon juice, extra virgin olive oil, and chopped parsley to create the dressing.
  7. Drizzle the lemon dressing over the salad and salmon.
  8. Toss gently to combine, ensuring the salad is well coated with the dressing.
  9. Serve immediately and enjoy your Grilled Salmon and Avocado Salad!
