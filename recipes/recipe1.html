Spaghetti Carbonara

A classic Italian pasta dish with creamy egg sauce, crispy pancetta, and parmesan cheese.

Ingredients:

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 150g pancetta
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. Fry pancetta until crispy.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, parmesan, salt, and black pepper.
  4. Drain spaghetti and immediately toss with egg mixture.
  5. Add pancetta and mix well.
  6. Serve hot with additional parmesan on top.
